About Us

We are a dynamic team of six passionate innovators united by our curiosity for space exploration and cutting-edge technology. Our project, Exoplanet Explorer, is our submission for the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2024. As students from Budhanilkantha School, we bring together a diverse set of skills ranging from frontend development and data analysis to astrophysics, machine learning, and game development. Our goal is to make space science accessible and exciting for everyone, especially children. Using real-time data from NASA, we are creating an engaging web app that teaches users about exoplanets through interactive lessons, quizzes, and short-form videos. By blending education with entertainment, our project aims to inspire the next generation of space enthusiasts. We believe that learning about the universe should be as fun and addictive as playing a game, and with Exoplanet Explorer, that’s exactly what we aim to achieve. We are excited to collaborate, learn, and innovate at this year’s hackathon!

The Learning App

Our app gives you a hands-on experience, teaching you about distant worlds, their atmospheres, and potential for life. Learn at your own pace, explore new discoveries, and become an exoplanet explorer!
